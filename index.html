about ch4 us

Purpose

ch4 us is a dynamic web tool that uses cutting-edge statistical analysis to accurately predict leakage zones within eog facilities, reducing the risk of accidental methane emissions.

Usage

The map allows users to visualize potential leakage points, the chart shows the status of each individual sensor, and the reporting feature allows the user to identify faulty equipment.

Accuracy

This model uses Gaussian Physics in order to accurately predict the dispersion of Methane gas given wind speed. It utilizes this calculation to trace the likely source of the gas leak.

Mission

We are committed to reducing our atmospheric emissions and creating a safe and transparent workplace for all of our employees. We strive to continuously improve our model and display up-to-date information.
